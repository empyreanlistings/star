About us

Find out why we built the company and what we are great at

Hi/ Mabuhay!

We are a team of experienced, qualified professionals passionate about bringing dream homes to life.

Combining our love for modern architecture, smart design, and cutting-edge technology, we specialize in building beautiful, energy-efficient homes in the Philippines.

Our projects are managed from concept to completion using Kai & Isla's proprietary technology, giving clients real-time visibility and peace of mind. With daily on-site updates, you’re always connected to the progress of your build.

Headquartered in El Nido, Palawan, we proudly serve clients across Puerto Princesa, El Nido and the Greater Northern Region.

Obsessed with Quality

We use modern building materials such as AAC blocks when constructing external and internal walls for our Homes. In particular, we use AAC for its high performance value versus traditional concrete hollow blocks.

AAC makes our homes energy efficient, earthquake proof, termite proof, fire proof and above all, look incredible with minimal rendering requirement, giving you more useable space inside.

We are nothing without our Team

We care about our employees. We provide safety inductions, we monitor health and safety at our sites and we provide incentives to retain our people and reward for high-standards of work.

Management Team

Ryan Dagomboy, Engr, CEO

Qualified Civil Engineer with 25+ years experience

Ryan brings 15 years of experience in high-rise structural engineering to the residential construction market, where he has since completed over 20 homes. Passionate, dedicated, and detail-oriented, Ryan combines his strong work ethic with a sharp eye for design to deliver exceptional, high-quality homes for our clients.

As a licensed Real Estate Broker, he also offers expert guidance and support for property transactions, making him a valuable partner from concept to completion.

Aica Silva Ramos, COO

Contracts & Construction Administration with 7+ years international experience

Aica is the queen of organization—keeping our paperwork in order, payments on time, and deliveries running like clockwork. With a sharp eye for detail and a calm, can-do attitude, she makes sure nothing slips through the cracks.

Before moving into Construction Management in the UAE, Aica was a qualified US-registered nurse. Now based in the stunning surroundings of El Nido, Palawan, she’s happily swapped scrubs for spreadsheets. Aica is also fluent in both English and Tagalog, making her an essential bridge between our team, clients, and local partners.

Steven Bradford, CTO

Chief Technology Officer & Systems Architect

Steven leads our technology vision, building the digital tools that power real-time project tracking, client dashboards, and automated workflows. With a background in software engineering and construction tech, he ensures our proprietary platform is secure, scalable, and intuitive.

From AI-powered progress prediction to mobile-first site reporting, Steven’s systems keep clients informed and teams aligned—delivering transparency at every step.
