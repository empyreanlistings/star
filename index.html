    PALAWAN

    THE WORLD'S BEST ISLAND

    Palawan isn't just beautiful — it's consistently ranked as one of the World's Best Islands.


    El Nido, Palawan boasts towering limestone karst cliffs rising dramatically from turquoise waters, hidden emerald lagoons, secret white-sand beaches in Bacuit Bay, and some of the Philippines' most vibrant marine life—including colorful coral reefs, sea turtles, and tropical fish—while maintaining a relaxed, small-town charm that perfectly blends thrilling island-hopping adventures with serene tropical escapism.

    Whether you're looking for investment opportunities, a holiday home, or a permanent move, Palawan delivers a lifestyle that's healthier, calmer, and closer to nature.

    More About Living in Palawan

    How to Get to Palawan

    Direct international flights to Puerto Princesa International Airport (PPS) are not currently available, with the airport primarily handling domestic routes; travelers from abroad typically connect through major Philippine hubs like Manila. Daily flights from Manila (about 75 minutes), Cebu (about 80 minutes), and Clark (about 85 minutes) provide convenient access. El Nido Airport (ENI), also known as Lio Airport, is fully operational with expanded services, including new direct flights from Cebu (launched October 26, 2025) and Clark (launched March 30, 2025), drastically cutting travel time to northern Palawan to as little as 70-90 minutes from those cities. San Vicente Airport has been open since 2018 and now features regular flights, such as Cebu Pacific's route from Cebu (launched October 2024), further improving connectivity to central Palawan beaches and attractions

    Local Amenities & Infrastructure

    Modern hospitals, international schools, high-speed fiber internet (up to 500 Mbps), malls, organic markets, yoga studios, dive centers, and a growing number of co-working spaces.

    Events & Lifestyle

    Baragatan Festival, Underground River tours, full-moon parties in Nacpan, international kiteboarding competitions, farm-to-table dinners, wellness retreats, and a vibrant expat community.
